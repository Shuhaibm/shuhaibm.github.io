Shuhaib Mehri

PhD @ UIUC

I am a PhD student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, advised by Dilek Hakkani-Tur.

I am interested in building user-centric agents that can effectively interact with users. My current work explores (1) multi-turn amd mixed initiative training, (2) continual learning across multiple sessions, and (3) reliable and realistic user simulation.

Before starting my PhD, I earned my BSc from the University of British Columbia, where I was fortunate enough to work with professors Vered Shwartz and Giuseppe Carenini. Additionally, I completed internships at iClinic Systems, Amazon (x3), and Clir Renewables.

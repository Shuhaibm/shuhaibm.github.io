Shuhaib Mehri

PhD @ UIUC

I am a PhD student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, advised by Dilek Hakkani-Tur.

My primary research interest lies in the natural language processing field. In the past, I have worked on modeling discourse parsing, information extraction, and evaluation of generative tasks.

Previously, I earned my BSc from the University of British Columbia, where I had the opportunity to work with professors Vered Shwartz and Giuseppe Carenini.
